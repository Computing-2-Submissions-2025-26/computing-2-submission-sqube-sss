MEMORY SPRINT

A two-player gesture-memory race

How to play

  1. Take turns. On your turn, pick a difficulty (1-5).
  2. A sequence of hand-gestures will flash on screen — memorise it.
  3. When "GO!" appears, perform the sequence using keys Q W E R T.
  4. Match the sequence correctly to move forward by your chosen difficulty (e.g. difficulty 3 = move 3 squares).
  5. Watch out for ❓ mystery squares — they're either bonuses or setbacks!
  6. First to the finish line wins!

Webcam mode tips

  • Hold each gesture steady for ~3 seconds — the game samples votes during a window and picks the most-seen gesture.
  • A visible progress bar shows your current gesture slot (e.g. "Gesture 2 of 3") and how much time is left in the window.
  • Good lighting and a plain background help the model classify correctly.
  • The model is trained on the developer's hands — accuracy may vary on different setups.
